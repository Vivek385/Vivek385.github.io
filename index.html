About Me

I am a dedicated and certified Automotive Cybersecurity Architect with a proven track record in designing and implementing robust security solutions for the connected vehicle ecosystem. My expertise lies in embedding security throughout the Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC), from initial threat modeling to final validation. I thrive on collaborating with cross-functional teams, including OEMs and Tier-1 suppliers, to translate complex security requirements into tangible, secure-by-design products. My goal is to stay at the forefront of automotive security, tackling emerging threats and contributing to a safer future for mobility.
